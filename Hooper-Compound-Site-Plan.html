WHAT THE WORDS MEAN
Nobody should have to Google while reading the family plan
ADU
Accessory Dwelling Unit — a smaller, separate home on the same lot as a main house. NC law (SB 495) says every town must allow at least one.
USDA 538
Guaranteed Rural Rental Housing Loan — government-backed mortgage for rural rental projects. 90% LTV, 40-year terms. The LLC can borrow.
CDBG
Community Development Block Grant — federal money for public infrastructure. The town applies, not us. 70% must benefit low-to-moderate income residents.
LIHTC
Low-Income Housing Tax Credit — 9% tax credit on qualifying workforce units for 10 years. Reduces project cost significantly.
LLC
Limited Liability Company — protects personal assets. If someone sues the property, they can only go after the LLC, not your house, car, or bank account.
FORTIFIED Gold
IBHS certification for hurricane-proof construction — impact windows, reinforced roof, continuous load path. Saves 25-50% on wind insurance.
Collateral
Something of value (our land) pledged to secure a loan. The bank holds a lien until the loan is paid off. Our land equity replaces the down payment.
Workforce Housing
Rental housing for people earning 60-120% of area median income — teachers, nurses, tradespeople. Not subsidized housing. Quality homes at fair rent.
